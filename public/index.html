Refined Woodworks is a small, one-person workshop in Kansas City that handcrafts custom furniture with a focus on quality, attention to detail, and timeless design. I strive to create functional and beautiful pieces by working with only the finest materials and using traditional techniques to ensure each piece created is durable and will last for generations. At Refined Woodworks, I believe that furniture should tell a story and enhance the character of any space it occupies.
